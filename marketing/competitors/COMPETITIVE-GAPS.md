## Messaging Gaps

### 1. Producer-Specific Workflow — NO ONE OWNS THIS
Every competitor says "split any song." Nobody talks about:
- Sampling workflow (extract a vocal chop, pitch it, chop it)
- DJ workflow (create acapellas for live sets)
- Remix workflow (isolate stems to rebuild a track)
- Beat-making workflow (grab a drum break, isolate the snare)

**44Stems angle:** "Built by producers, for producers. Not a toy — a tool."

### 2. Audio Quality / Model Transparency — NO ONE TALKS ABOUT THIS
- LALAL.AI says "studio-quality" but never shows proof
- No competitor mentions SDR, SIR, or any objective quality metric
- No competitor lets you compare models or choose your separation engine

**44Stems angle:** "We show you the numbers. SDR scores on every model. You pick the best one for your track."

### 3. Format & Workflow Details — IGNORED BY ALL
- No competitor mentions AIFF support (critical for Logic Pro users)
- No competitor mentions batch processing
- No competitor mentions lossless output quality
- No competitor talks about credit transparency (know cost before you split)

**44Stems angle:** "AIFF in, AIFF out. Know your credit cost before you split. Batch process your entire sample library."

### 4. Price Transparency — EVERYONE HIDES IT
- LALAL.AI buries pricing behind sign-up
- Moises pushes app subscription
- Nobody shows pricing in their ads

**44Stems angle:** Show pricing directly in ads. "3 free splits. Then $X/month. No surprises."
