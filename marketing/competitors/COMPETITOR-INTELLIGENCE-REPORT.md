# Competitor Ad Intelligence Report — Stem Separation Market

**Date:** 2026-04-09
**Source:** Meta Ad Library (primary), Google Ads Transparency Center, TikTok Creative Center
**Market:** AI stem separation / vocal removal

---

## Executive Summary

The stem separation ad market is dominated by **Moises.ai** (690+ active Meta ads) and **ElevenLabs** (1000+ active Meta ads). **LALAL.AI** runs a smaller but focused campaign (25 active Meta ads). Smaller players like **eMastered** and **Fadr** are emerging. The market is heavily skewed toward Meta/Instagram — Google Search presence is minimal for direct competitors.

**Key finding:** There is a massive gap for a producer-focused, quality-first brand. All competitors target broad audiences with generic "split any song" messaging. None speak specifically to music producers' workflows.

### 3. ElevenLabs — Adjacent Competitor (Vocal/Audio AI)

**Meta Ads:** 1000+ active ads
**Platforms:** Facebook, Instagram, Audience Network, Messenger, Threads
**Active since:** Ongoing, massive scale

#### Ad Copy Analysis
- Focuses on voice cloning, text-to-speech, dubbing
- Vocal isolation is a secondary feature, not primary messaging
- Targets content creators, developers, businesses — not musicians

#### Creative Strategy
- **Visual style:** Clean, tech-forward, product demos
- **Formats:** Mix of video and static, heavy carousel usage
- **Creative volume:** Extremely high — 1000+ active ads
- **Multi-audience:** Different creatives for different segments

#### Relevance to 44Stems
- ElevenLabs is not a direct competitor for stem separation
- Their "vocal remover" feature is a small subset of their offering
- They compete on brand awareness in the broader "AI audio" space
- Their massive ad spend raises the floor for audio AI advertising CPMs
